Scam Alerts

Stay informed about common online scams and learn how to protect yourself while searching for jobs, freelance work, and digital opportunities.

🚨 Stay Alert Online

Online opportunities can create great possibilities, but scammers also use fake offers to steal money and personal information. Always verify before you trust an opportunity.

Common Online Scams

Fake Job Offers

Be careful with employers who ask for registration fees, training payments, or deposits before offering work. Genuine employers normally do not charge you to get hired.

Fake Investment Schemes

Avoid platforms promising guaranteed profits or unrealistic returns with little risk. Always research before investing money.

Phishing Messages

Never share passwords, PINs, verification codes, or banking details through unknown links, emails, or messages.

Fake Giveaways and Prizes

Scammers may claim you won something and request payment or personal details before releasing your "prize".

Identity Theft

Protect your personal documents and only share information with verified and trusted organizations.

🚩 Red Flags to Watch For

  • Promises of guaranteed income with no effort.
  • Requests for upfront payments before work starts.
  • Pressure to make quick decisions.
  • Requests for passwords, PINs, or verification codes.
  • Unknown websites with no company information.
  • Offers that seem too good to be true.

How to Stay Safe

  • Research companies and platforms before joining.
  • Use official websites and verified contact details.
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available.
  • Keep your passwords private and secure.
  • Ask questions before sharing personal information.
