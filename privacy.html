Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This page explains how EarnWise Africa handles information when you use our website.

Introduction

EarnWise Africa is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our website. We believe in transparency and responsible handling of information.

Information We Collect

You can browse EarnWise Africa without creating an account or providing personal information.

If you contact us or submit an opportunity, we may collect information such as your name, email address, or message so we can respond appropriately.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and feedback.
  • To review submitted opportunities.
  • To improve our website and user experience.
  • To provide educational content and trusted online opportunities.

Third-Party Websites

Our website may include links to third-party websites. These websites have their own privacy policies and practices. We encourage you to review them before sharing any personal information.

Keeping Your Information Safe

We encourage all visitors to use strong passwords, avoid sharing sensitive information unnecessarily, and be cautious when interacting with third-party websites.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as EarnWise Africa grows or new features are introduced. Any updates will be published on this page.
